OUR TEAM

At Texas Guadaloop, we are seeking driven, ambitious, and creative students to join our team. Our achievements are a direct result of the talent and dedication of the students involved. Whether you’re looking to enhance your engineering, research, and business skills or gain leadership experience, we provide the resources to help you excel. This isn’t just an engineering project; it’s a real-world initiative with business challenges, corporate responsibility, and cutting-edge technology. Success depends on structured management, clear communication, and the brilliant minds behind the project. We operate as a unified team and can’t wait for you to be part of it. Our mission is to develop students into professionals through hands-on work with groundbreaking technology. While recruitment for the current semester is closed, we look forward to welcoming new team members in Spring 2024.
