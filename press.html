Green Fund Texas 2024

We were recently announced as one of the winners of the Green Fund in spring 2024. We will be receiving a 26K grant from them during the Fall semester. This is great news for Texas Guadaloop and demonstrates reinforced support from UT Austin towards our project and more generally towards sustainability. This money will assist our research for cleaner and more efficient transportation technology. The Green Fund is a competitive grant program funded by UT Austin tuition fees to support sustainability-related projects and initiatives proposed by university students, faculty or staff. Each student contributes $5 per semester and $2.50 per summer session to the Green Fund. Funds are awarded each May through an annual grant competition. A student majority committee solicits and reviews proposals and awards Green Fund grants.
