Become a Sponsor

Our team is powered by generous financial and in-kind contributions (tax deductible). By donating to Texas Guadaloop, you are choosing to not only invest in cutting-edge green technology, but also in a group of innovators with the potential to change the world.

Contributions may come in the form of financial gifts, but also in the form of materials and parts. Please feel free to contact us for more details at uthyperloopengineering@gmail.com!

Our specific needs for the upcoming year include:

  • $63,200 — Research and construction of a functional, trailblazing pod
  • $18,000 — Development of complementary test track
  • $94,800 — Competition-related expenses

Learn more about our contribution levels (described below) and get started on forming a mutually-beneficial partnership with Texas Guadaloop!

Donate Today

Can't be a sponsor but still want to contribute to Texas Guadaloop? Show your support by donating to Texas Guadaloop. You can make a donation by clicking the donate button and selecting "Hyperloop Engineering at UT (HEUT)" under special information. 100% of donations go into research and development of hyperloop technologies.

Our Hornraiser is also currently active! Here, you can learn more about our goals to attend European Hyperloop Week (EHW) next summer, and make donations that will go directly towards financing our team's presence at the most prestigious collegiate hyperloop competition in the world.
