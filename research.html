Research Team

What does the research team do?

Developing research about the Hyperloop to determine the applicability/sustainability of the Hyperloop in society. The Research Team acts as a bridge between Engineering and Business Teams, focusing on the reality of Hyperloop implementation both in terms of future use cases and major limitations/barriers.

Our Main Responsibilities

  • Develop Research to compete in EHW/Hyperloop Global
  • Publish Research about Hyperloop
  • Creating innovative ideas for the Hyperloop
  • Learn how hyperloop can be applied in the real world

Skills

Technical Writing, Conducting Market Research, Presenting
