About Us

What is Hyperloop...

Hyperloop represents the future of transportation—an innovative fifth mode that will soon move people at unprecedented speeds, offering greater safety than air travel, more affordability than traditional methods, and a sustainable alternative for the planet. At Guadaloop, we are dedicated to advancing hyperloop technology, driving a wide range of operations with the shared mission of making this revolutionary system a reality for the world.

...And who are we?

While our work may seem purely technical, Guadaloop is far more than an engineering team. We are a diverse group of talented individuals from various disciplines, including engineering, computer science, business, economics, math, physics, psychology, and more. Our organization is divided into three main teams: Engineering, Research, and Business. Together, we see ourselves as a coalition of researchers, engineers, and entrepreneurs, united by a shared mission to transform the future of transportation. As a multidisciplinary team of driven, passionate future leaders, we are relentless in our pursuit of innovation and change.
